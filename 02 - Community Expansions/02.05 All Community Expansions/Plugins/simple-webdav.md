# Simple WebDAV Sync

Plugin ID: `simple-webdav`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/gghyoo/simple-webdav) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=simple-webdav)
