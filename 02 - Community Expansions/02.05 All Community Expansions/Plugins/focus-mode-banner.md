# Focus Mode Banner

Plugin ID: `focus-mode-banner`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/adrianofontanari/obsidian-focus-mode-banner) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=focus-mode-banner)
Developed by: [[adrianofontanari]]
Mobile compatible: [[Mobile-compatible plugins|Yes]]

Activates a Focus Mode: a bold banner appears every time you open a note different from the one where you started your focus session.
