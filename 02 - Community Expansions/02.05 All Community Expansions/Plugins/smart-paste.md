# Smart Paste

Plugin ID: `smart-paste`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/konstantinosgkilas/smart-paste) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=smart-paste)
Developed by: [[konstantinosgkilas]]
Mobile compatible: [[Mobile-compatible plugins|Yes]]

Automatically cleans and transforms clipboard content on paste — converts HTML to markdown, fetches link titles, strips trackers, and normalizes whitespace.
