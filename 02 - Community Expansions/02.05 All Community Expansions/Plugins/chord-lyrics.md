# Chord Lyrics

Plugin ID: `chord-lyrics`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/nevernotmove/obsidian-chordlyrics) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=chord-lyrics)
