# Click Image to Zoom

Plugin ID: `sfb-click-image-to-zoom`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/saltyfireball/obsidian-click-image-to-zoom) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=sfb-click-image-to-zoom)
Developed by: [[saltyfireball]]
Mobile compatible: [[Mobile-compatible plugins|Yes]]

Click any image in your notes to open it in a full-size zoomable modal. Supports scroll-to-zoom, drag-to-pan, and per-note frontmatter control.
