# Inbox Zero

Plugin ID: `inbox-zero`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/yuzameone/Inbox-Zero) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=inbox-zero)
Developed by: [[yuzameone]]
Mobile compatible: [[Mobile-compatible plugins|Yes]]

Dynamic checklists: tag files with #tags, view them as checkboxes in your notes, and delete them permanently with one click. Drag-and-drop ordering preserved across sessions. Keep your vault clean and your inbox at zero.
