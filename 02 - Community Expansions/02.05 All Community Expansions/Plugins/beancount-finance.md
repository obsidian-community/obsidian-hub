# Beancount Ledger

Plugin ID: `beancount-finance`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/mkshp-dev/obsidian-finance-plugin) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=beancount-finance)
Developed by: [[mkshp-dev]]
Mobile compatible: [[Desktop-only plugins|No]]

Manage your Beancount ledger from your vault. Supports live BQL queries, transaction management, net worth tracking, balance sheets, income statements, and automated price fetching.
