# Force Reading Mode

Plugin ID: `force-reading-mode`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/shubhmadhavan/obsidian-force-reading-mode) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=force-reading-mode)
