# Zotero Research Assistant

Plugin ID: `zotero-redisearch-rag`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/jmiba/zotero-redisearch-rag) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=zotero-redisearch-rag)
