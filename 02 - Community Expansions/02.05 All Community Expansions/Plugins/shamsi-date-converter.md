# Shamsi Date Converter

Plugin ID: `shamsi-date-converter`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/milad-s5/obsidian-shamsi-date-converter) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=shamsi-date-converter)
Developed by: [[milad-s5]]
Mobile compatible: [[Mobile-compatible plugins|Yes]]

Automatically converts Gregorian dates to Shamsi (Jalali/Persian) dates. Supports multiple date pairs (start/end dates). Updates automatically when source dates change.
